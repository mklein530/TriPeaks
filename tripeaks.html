Tri-Peaks Solitaire



How To Play

The goal of the game is to clear all the peaks. Cards can be played if their value is one less than or one greater than that of the current card on top of the waste pile. If no displayed (face up) card can be played, you can flip a card from the stock pile and continue playing from there. You have a time limit of 2:30 to clear the peaks.

Scoring System

The first card played is worth 100 points, and each consecutive card played is worth 200 points. A bonus is added to your score at the end of the game depending on how fast you clear the peaks. The bonus begins at 20000 points and decrements by 200 each second.
